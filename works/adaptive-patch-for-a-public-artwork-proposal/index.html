Adaptive patch for a public artwork proposal

2020-01-04 · video

In this piece, GPS coordinates are used as musical events and modifiers. This marks my first foray into spatial computing, courtesy of Holonic's partnership with Bose. The Bose AR system enabled true, non-optical positioning and Auditory AR, in this case gaze-controlled spatial music.
