Early work moved between photographic series, editorial commissions, and documentation. Much of it was concerned with atmosphere, framing, and the slow accumulation of material detail rather than with discrete narrative.

These projects also formed a practical base for later work in sound and systems, especially in how they approached process, staging, and the relation between event and trace.
