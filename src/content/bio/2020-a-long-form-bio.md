Archival work includes photography, audiovisual production, theatre, radio, and commissioned design. These projects belong to an earlier phase of practice, but they remain relevant as part of a longer interest in mediation, timing, atmosphere, and the shaping of attention.

- 2012: early experiments with analogue and hybrid graphic processes.
- 2016: transition toward mixed media and more explicitly time-based work.
