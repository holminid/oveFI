This piece was created with Ilmatila's aerialist Ilona Jäntti for Dutch Design Week 2020, together with Holonic's Mark Hakonen-Meddings.

In order to generate music from her dance, Ilona used five Movesense devices, one for each limb and one to detect posture and heart rate.
